This view looks southeasterly along State Street in Clarks Summit (approximate coordinates: N 41.494335°, W 75.703333°). The siding just visible at front left indicates the approach to the Clarks Summit station, which was located at the site of the current Post Office.

Visible at front right is Bunnell Hardware, founded in 1911 (the same year as the incorporation of Clarks Summit Borough). The store was family owned and continuously operated until its closure in December 2013.
